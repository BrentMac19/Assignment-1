Family Support Programs

Nearly 800,000 School-aged children have a parent serving in the Military, This brings an uproar into the family because children dont quite understand why the parent has to leave. Also the transition from Service member to civilian can be difficult for a Veteren. Through Veteren Support Programs provided by the WWP this transition becomes much easier because they help families of veterans reconnect through events that support family bonding an transitional skills, by providing space and time for veterens to spend with their love ones.



Warriors To Work

Approimently 53% of homeless Veterans have disailities. That is more than half! This is due to lack of support, of transition to a work enviornment. WWP provides a program called Warrior To Work, This program connects veterans with employers and resources for jobs. They also provide Career counseling, so veterans can find work That best fits their skill sets and allows them to smoothly transition into civilian life.



Mental Wellness

11-12% of service members are suffering from PTSD, This is a very difficult disablility to live with and can be very hard for a Veteran to over come without proper care. Veteran mental health programs are an important staple in a veterans journey to mental Wellness. WWP provides interactive programs, rehabilitation retreats, and free mental health counseling.
