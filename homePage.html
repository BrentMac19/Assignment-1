Wounded Warrior Project



The Wounded Warrior Project (WWP) is a Nonprofit organization dedicated to helping wounded veterans lead successful lives after their service. They partner with other charities to assist in this mission. WWP has provided a year long track program in the past that tries to help veterans transition into the workplace or college.
